Building your course together with our eLearning specialists

Each project presents its own unique set of learners and circumstances for moving content online. Our approach is to look at each project to identify the design and delivery techniques that are most appropriate for the context, the content, the needs and the target audience.

Our choice of instructional design techniques are based on many questions such as:
  • How can we make the content more engaging?
  • How can we give the learners the opportunity to apply their learning?
  • How can we help the learners transfer their learning to the workplace?
  • How do we need to evaluate learners?
